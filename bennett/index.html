Advantage Bennett University

The Times Group established Bennett University in 2016 with a vision to provide quality education and establish itself as one of the best private 
          universities in India to impart interdisciplinary education. It was established by the Act of the State Legislature of Uttar Pradesh as a 
          private university.

          The six schools with 30+ programs and 70+ leading specialisations in Engineering, Management, Media, Law and Liberal Arts have positioned it as 
          one of the top universities in India. This has made it a sought-after destination for fortune companies and leading corporates in a short period,
           picking talents with prominent placement records.
          
          The serene 68-acre campus is extremely popular among the students as the university encourages 'fun learning'  and 'life outside the classroom'   
           with a vision of innovation.
